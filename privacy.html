Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to REMAKE ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website (remake.beauty), join our waitlist, or use our mobile application (the "App").

2. Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following data about you:

  • Waitlist Data: If you join our waitlist, we collect your email address and any preferences you share.
  • Usage Data: When you use our website, we automatically collect basic analytics data (like IP addresses, browser types, and interactions) to improve user experience.
  • App Data (Upon Launch): When the App launches, we will collect data related to your product scans, cosmetic preferences, and saved shade matches to provide you with personalized beauty coaching.
  • Face and Camera Data: To provide personalized shade matching and facial feature analysis, REMAKE requires access to your device's camera and photos. Images processed by our AI (including Llama 3.2 90B) are analyzed in real-time. We do not store, sell, or share your Face Data with third parties for marketing purposes. Face Data is retained only as long as necessary to provide the shade-matching feature during your active session and is then immediately discarded from our active processing servers.
  • TrueDepth API / Depth Data: If we utilize Apple's TrueDepth API for accurate facial mapping, this data is processed entirely locally on your device or ephemerally on our secure backend and is never stored persistently.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your information strictly to provide and improve our services, including:

  • Sending you updates about the REMAKE launch and relevant cosmetic/skincare insights.
  • Providing you with accurate comedogenic ratings, toxic ingredient alerts, and shade matching.
  • Improving our algorithm to give you better makeup and skincare advice.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Our business model relies on direct app subscriptions, not selling your data.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party service providers (like email marketing platforms and analytics tools) to help us operate our business. These third parties have access to your personal data only to perform specific tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure a level of security appropriate to the risk, protecting your data against accidental or unlawful destruction, loss, alteration, or unauthorized access.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your personal data. If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at theremakeapp@gmail.com.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: theremakeapp@gmail.com
